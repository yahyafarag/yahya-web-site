Case Study

Custom CMMS Implementation

The Challenge

Reactive maintenance approaches and disjointed tracking across multiple branches.

The Approach & Action

Designed and deployed a customized Computerized Maintenance Management System.

The lack of visibility into maintenance operations caused excessive downtime. We standardized predictive routines and deployed a unified CMMS, ensuring all work orders and spare parts inventory were tracked in real-time.

The Result

Successfully integrated the system across 200+ operational facilities, significantly reducing downtime.
