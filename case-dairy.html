Case Study

Dairy Plant Restructuring

The Challenge

Aging infrastructure limiting production efficiency in a major dairy facility.

The Approach & Action

Led a comprehensive modernization project, upgrading automation systems and production lines.

Upgraded PLCs and SCADA systems, resolved hygienic design flaws, and retrained the engineering staff to handle the modernized high-speed dairy lines.

The Result

Enhanced overall equipment effectiveness (OEE) and ensured strict compliance with food safety standards.
